An e-learning system is a digital platform or environment designed for delivering educational content and facilitating learning activities over the internet. These systems are used to support both formal and informal education, offering tools for instruction, content distribution, assessment, communication, and collaboration. E-learning systems can be designed for a wide range of learners, including K-12 students, university students, and professionals seeking further training or certifications.

The goal of e-learning is to provide an effective, flexible, and accessible way of learning through digital platforms, making education available to a broader audience and improving the learning experience.
